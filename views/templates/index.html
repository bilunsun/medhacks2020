MedHacks 2020 - Personalized Medicine: Redefining Cancer Treatment
Project By: An Nan Pan, Bilun Sun, Mahad Janjua, Zheng Yu Cui
About
The interpretation of genetic mutations in sequenced cancer tumor is currently a long and tedious process, done manually based on clinical literature. The Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center has launched a challenge in conjunction with Kaggle for a Machine Learning program that can read clinical texts and classify genetic mutations automatically.
